My Hobby

My name is Moisés (with an accent mark on the letter E) I also go by MOY Moy (Rhymes with boy)

  • I was born and raised in Mexico. My family and I migrated to the US 30 years ago. I currently live in the Mission/Castro districts in San Francisco.
  • One of the things I enjoy the most, my hobby, is some interior design and includes mainly repurposing furniture and creating art pieces.
  • This hobby got me thru the toughest days during the pandemic as I was permanently in lockdown in my apartment.
  • It is not only entertaining but highly therapeutic to me.
  • I do accounting and small business consulting for a living. Doing this, taps into my left/right side of the brain or what is known as cognitive thinking.
  • I find used furniture on the streets or discarded pieces of art and I give them a new life.
  • Most of the ideas come from my head. However, I find most of my inspiration from many different artists. I am a highly visual individual. I really enjoy observing art in all forms. That eventually gives ideas that I adapt into my own.
